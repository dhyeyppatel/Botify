About RunMyBot

RunMyBot is a clean, beginner-friendly Telegram bot hosting platform. Paste your token, name your bot, and start adding commands—no servers, no DevOps.

Why RunMyBot?

  • Zero setup: works from your browser—create, start/stop, and manage bots easily.
  • Command editor: paste code snippets that run safely in a sandbox with ctx.
  • Guided docs: sample commands, token guide, and usage templates for faster building.
  • Scale later: upgrade for more bots, logs, and advanced features.

How it works (quick)

  1. Create a bot in Telegram via @BotFather and copy the token.
  2. Click Create New Bot on your dashboard, paste token & bot name.
  3. Open Commands, add commands using our snippets, and click Start.
